United Nations International Translation Day

作者 柴丽丝 / 翻译

2017 is a historic milestone for all professional interpreters and translators. Because the 71st session of the United Nations General Assembly unanimously adopted a resolution recognizing the role of professional translators in communicating and promoting peace, understanding and development among countries. In this resolution, the General Assembly also declared September 30 of each year as the United Nations International Translation Day. Language crosses the barriers of distance, boundary and time. The translation of literature and other specific cultures, such as the translation of subtitles and dubbing of foreign films, is different from localization. This type of translation aims to open a window for cultural origin to share local ideas and another worldview. Make people feel that they can understand another culture in their own language.

对所有职业口笔译工作者来说，2017年具有历史性的里程碑意义。因为第71届联合国大会一致通过了决议，承认职业译员在各国沟通和促进和平、理解、发展方面发挥的作用。在这份决议中，联大还宣布，将每年的9月30日确定为联合国国际翻译日。 语言跨越了距离、界限和时间的屏障。文学和其他特定文化的翻译，如外国电影的字幕和配音的翻译与本地化有所不同。这种类型的翻译旨在为源于文化打开窗户，分享当地的思想以及另外一种世界观。使人们体会到用自己的语言了解另一种文化。
